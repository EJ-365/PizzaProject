FAQ

    What is Instant Pizza?

Instant Pizza is a quick and convenient way to enjoy delicious pizzas at home. Our pizzas are pre-made, frozen, and can be cooked in just minutes.

    How do I prepare the pizzas?

Simply preheat your oven to the temperature indicated on the packaging, remove the pizza from its packaging, and bake for the recommended time. You can also use a microwave or air fryer for faster cooking.

    What types of pizzas do you offer?

We offer a variety of pizzas, including classic cheese, pepperoni, veggie, and specialty options. Seasonal flavors are also available.

    Are your pizzas suitable for dietary restrictions?

We have gluten-free and vegan options available. Please check the packaging for specific dietary information.

    Where can I buy Instant Pizza?

You can purchase our pizzas online through our website or at select grocery stores. Check our store locator for specific locations.

    How should I store the pizzas?

Keep the pizzas frozen until you are ready to cook them. Do not refreeze after cooking.

    What is your return policy?

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer service within 30 days for a refund or exchange.

    Do you offer bulk orders for events?

Yes! We offer catering options for events. Please contact us directly for more information on pricing and availability.

    Can I customize my pizza?

Currently, our pizzas come pre-made. However, you can add your favorite toppings before cooking for a personalized touch!

    How can I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team via email at support@instantpizza.com or by calling our hotline at 1-800-111-PIZZA.
